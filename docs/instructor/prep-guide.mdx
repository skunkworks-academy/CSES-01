# Instructor Prep Guide

## Instructor readiness

Before delivery, confirm:

- Azure demo tenant or customer tenant access.
- Microsoft Defender for Cloud availability.
- Permissions for environment review.
- Demo resources for storage, app services, containers, and AI scenarios where possible.
- Safety boundaries for live customer environment review.

## Recommended delivery approach

| Phase | Instructor action |
|---|---|
| Opening | Confirm customer objectives and risk appetite. |
| Strategy | Facilitate security outcomes mapping. |
| Posture | Demonstrate Defender for Cloud overview, recommendations, and Secure Score. |
| Protection | Select two workload protection plans for deeper walkthrough. |
| AI | Discuss AI identity, data, guardrails, and monitoring controls. |
| Close | Produce operational readiness summary and next steps. |

## Demo safety guidance

- Do not change customer production settings without explicit approval.
- Use read-only review wherever possible.
- Record findings without exposing secrets or sensitive data.
- Keep screenshots sanitized.
- Confirm who owns remediation tasks before assigning actions.
