# Lab 02: CSPM Risk Prioritization

## Scenario

Your organization has many Defender for Cloud recommendations. You need to prioritize them based on exploitability, asset criticality, compliance impact, and identity exposure.

## Tasks

### Task 1 — Review recommendations

1. Open **Recommendations** in Microsoft Defender for Cloud.
2. Sort by severity or risk.
3. Select three findings that affect critical assets.

### Task 2 — Evaluate risk context

For each finding, answer:

- Is the affected resource internet-facing?
- Does the resource contain sensitive data?
- Is there an identity privilege concern?
- Does the finding affect compliance?
- Is the remediation low effort or high effort?

## Deliverable

Submit a prioritized list of three recommendations with business justification.
